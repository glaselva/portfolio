About Me

My mission is to simplify the web approach. I handle the technical aspects, so the user can focus on their business. I guide them through the initial stages, highlighting their story and transforming the site into a clear, useful, and truly personal tool.

This site collects some personal and demo projects that represent my approach to web development.

What I can do for you

  • Modern showcase websites
  • Clean, professional design
  • Fully responsive layouts
  • Databases implementation
  • Fast loading performance
  • Multi‑language, multi‑page websites
  • Basic on-page SEO
  • No monthly fees

Approach to Development

  • Analysis and discovery: understand the client's history, personality, and needs
  • Creative concept: define a customized design, structure, and functionality
  • Prototyping: create sketches and wireframes to validate the user experience
  • Technical development: build the site, integrating aesthetics, clarity, and usability across all devices
  • Delivery and support: accompany the client until they are completely independent in managing the site

The goal is to create solid, understandable, and functional projects over time.
